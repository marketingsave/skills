# End-to-End Examples

## Example 1: Cohort — "Launch Your First Digital Product" (6 Weeks)

```
Week 1: Idea Validation — validate with 10 real conversations
Week 2: Minimum Viable Product — simplest version that delivers value
Week 3: Sales Page — copy that converts (PAS framework)
Week 4: Launch Sequence — 5-email sequence
Week 5: Launch Week — daily action items
Week 6: Post-Launch — analyze, collect testimonials, plan next
```

## Example 2: Cohort — "Content Marketing Mastery" (4 Weeks)

```
Week 1: Content Strategy — pillars, audience, cadence
Week 2: Writing Systems — templates and workflows
Week 3: Distribution — repurposing engine
Week 4: Measurement — track what matters, double down on winners
```

## Example 3: Mastermind — Revenue-Focused Solopreneur

```
Members: 6 solopreneurs earning $5K-$15K/month
Cadence: Bi-weekly, 90 minutes on Zoom
Price: $300/month, 6-month commitment
Format: 2 hot seats per meeting, rotating schedule
Focus: Revenue growth, offer optimization, systems
```

## Example 4: Mastermind — Free Community

```
Members: 8 members from an online community
Cadence: Monthly, 2 hours
Price: Free (community benefit)
Format: 3 hot seats per meeting, peer-facilitated (rotating leader)
Focus: General business challenges, peer support
```

## Example 5: Hybrid — 8-Week Group Coaching

```
Format: Weekly 60-75 min live group call + Circle community + async assignments
Group: 12 members, $849/person
Structure: Teaching + hot seats each call, accountability pairs between calls
Graduation: Personal action plan + group celebration in week 8
```
