# Large Templates

## Group Agreement (mastermind template — adapt for other modes)

```
By joining this group, I commit to:
- Attending [X]% of scheduled meetings (minimum [Y] of [Z])
- Arriving on time and staying for the full session
- Preparing for my hot seat using the prep form
- Keeping all group discussions confidential
- Giving honest, constructive feedback
- Following through on my stated commitments
- Communicating in advance if I must miss

Violation of confidentiality or repeated no-shows → removal.
```

## Hot Seat Prep Form (mastermind + hybrid)

```
**Your challenge in one sentence:**
**Background context (3-4 sentences):**
**What you have already tried:**
**What kind of help you want:** (Brainstorming / Feedback / Accountability / Connections)
**Ideal outcome from this hot seat:**
```

## Community Guidelines (all modes)

- Introduce yourself using the provided template
- Share wins and lessons regularly
- Give feedback when asked (SBI framework: Situation, Behavior, Impact)
- No pitching or selling to other participants
- What is shared in the group stays in the group

## Launch Sequence (mastermind)

```
Week -4: Open applications, promote to audience
Week -2: Review applications, select members, send invitations
Week -1: Onboarding call — intros, expectations, schedule
Week 1:  First official meeting — light hot seats, relationship building
Month 1-2: Build rhythm
Month 3: Mid-point check-in — is the group working? Adjust.
Month 6: Close cohort — celebrate, collect feedback, offer renewal
```

## Facilitation Tips (all modes)

- Start on time even if someone is late
- Keep strict time on hot seats — use a visible timer
- Redirect tangents: "Great topic for your hot seat next time"
- Call on quiet members by name: "Sarah, your take?"
- End every meeting with stated commitments — accountability is the product
- **Mastermind specifically**: the facilitator manages time/energy/structure, not content. Do not do all the advising.
