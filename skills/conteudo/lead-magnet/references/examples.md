## Example Lead Magnet Content — "The Ultimate Content Repurposing Checklist"

```
# The Ultimate Content Repurposing Checklist

## Who This Is For
Solo content creators who publish on 2+ platforms and want to stop writing
everything from scratch every single week.

## The Checklist

### Step 1: Pick Your Source Piece
- [ ] Choose your best-performing content from the last 30 days (highest
      engagement, most saves, most replies)
- [ ] Confirm the core idea can be explained in one sentence
- [ ] Identify at least 3 supporting points, stats, or examples in the piece

### Step 2: Extract the Building Blocks
- [ ] Write down the ONE big takeaway in a single sentence
- [ ] Pull out 3-5 standalone quotes or bold claims
- [ ] List every specific tip, step, or tactic mentioned
- [ ] Note any stories, anecdotes, or case studies

### Step 3: Repurpose to Each Platform
- [ ] Twitter/X: Turn the big takeaway into a hook tweet, supporting points
      into a thread (5-8 tweets)
- [ ] LinkedIn: Write a 150-250 word post opening with a counterintuitive
      hook, ending with a question
- [ ] Instagram: Pull the boldest quote for a carousel cover, use tips
      as carousel slides (5-7 slides)
- [ ] Email: Write a personal intro (2 sentences), share the core insight,
      end with one CTA
- [ ] Short-form video: Record a 30-45 second script — hook, one key point,
      CTA

### Step 4: Schedule and Stagger
- [ ] Post the Twitter thread first (Day 1)
- [ ] Post LinkedIn the next morning (Day 2)
- [ ] Send the email on Day 3
- [ ] Post Instagram carousel on Day 4
- [ ] Post video on Day 5

## Quick Reference
| Platform | Format | Length | Post Order |
|----------|--------|--------|-----------|
| Twitter/X | Thread | 5-8 tweets | Day 1 |
| LinkedIn | Text post | 150-250 words | Day 2 |
| Email | Newsletter | 200-300 words | Day 3 |
| Instagram | Carousel | 5-7 slides | Day 4 |
| Video | Short-form | 30-45 seconds | Day 5 |

## What to Do Next
Want the full system? Join the Content Systems newsletter for weekly
repurposing breakdowns and templates: [newsletter link]
```
