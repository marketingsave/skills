# Case Study — Worked Examples

Four end-to-end walkthroughs covering long-form, short-form, one-pager, and anonymous/recovery scenarios.

## Example 1: Marketing Agency — E-Commerce Revenue Growth (Long-Form, ~950 words)

**User says:** "I want to write a case study about Thornberry Goods, an e-commerce candle brand. They were doing $12K/month with no paid ads and a basic Shopify store. We redesigned the store, ran Facebook and Instagram ads, and set up email automations. After 6 months they hit $52K/month."

**Phase 1 extraction:**

```
**Client:** Thornberry Goods (E-commerce, handmade candles, DTC)
**Contact:** Jake Thornberry, Founder
**Before:** $12K/month revenue, no paid ads, basic Shopify template,
no email list. Jake handling everything himself. Plateaued for 8 months.
Tried boosting Instagram posts ($200/month) with no return.
**Solution:** Store redesign, Facebook/Instagram ads, email automation
flows (welcome, abandoned cart, post-purchase), monthly reporting.
**Timeline:** 6 months
**Results:**
- Revenue: $12K/month to $52K/month (340% increase)
- ROAS: 4.2x on paid ads
- Email revenue: 28% of total (from 0%)
- Conversion rate: 1.1% to 3.4%
- Email list: 0 to 8,200 subscribers
**Quote:** "They completely changed my business. I went from wondering
if I could keep the lights on to hiring my first two employees."
```

**Phase 2 outline:** Headline "340% Revenue Growth for a Handmade Candle Brand in 6 Months." Snapshot: 4 metrics. Challenge: 8-month plateau, failed boosted posts, founder burnout. Solution: 3 phases (store redesign, ad launch, email automation). Results: 5 metrics with before/after + unexpected win (first hires). CTA links to e-commerce growth services.

**Phase 3 output:** Full long-form case study, approximately 950 words, following the SCAR template in `templates.md`.

**Phase 4:** Saved to `thornberry-goods-case-study.md`. Offered short-form derivative for social media and a one-pager for sales attachments.

## Example 2: Web Designer — Local Bakery Online Orders (Short-Form, ~400 words)

**User says:** "I redesigned the website for Rise & Shine Bakery. They had an old WordPress site with no online ordering. I built them a new site with an order form and their orders went up 85% in the first month. Can you make this a short case study for my portfolio?"

**Phase 1 extraction:**

```
**Client:** Rise & Shine Bakery (Local bakery, single location)
**Contact:** Maria Chen, Owner
**Before:** Outdated WordPress site, no online ordering. Customers
called or walked in. Losing weekend catering orders to competitors.
**Solution:** Complete redesign with online ordering, updated menu
with photos, mobile-responsive, Google Business Profile optimization.
**Timeline:** 3 weeks
**Results:**
- Online orders: 0 to 47/week (85% total order increase)
- Weekend catering inquiries: +120%
- Phone orders: -40% (shifted to online, freeing staff)
**Quote:** "I used to spend half my morning answering the phone
for orders. Now they come in while I sleep."
```

**Phase 2:** Short-form, ~400 words. Headline: "85% Order Increase for a Local Bakery in 30 Days." Snapshot: 3 metrics. Compressed challenge/solution/results using short-form template.

**Phase 4:** Saved to `rise-and-shine-case-study.md`. Suggested placements: portfolio page + PDF one-pager for proposals.

## Example 3: B2B Consultant — Pipeline Generation (One-Pager, ~250 words)

**User says:** "Need a PDF one-pager for a sales meeting tomorrow. Client was NorthPeak Advisory — we ran an outbound campaign and generated $47K in qualified pipeline in 90 days. Previously they were relying on referrals and had gone 4 months without a new client."

**Phase 1 extraction:**

```
**Client:** NorthPeak Advisory (B2B consulting, 8-person firm)
**Contact:** Darren Voss, Managing Partner
**Before:** Referral-only pipeline. 4 consecutive months without a new
client signed. No outbound motion. Had tried hiring a BDR in 2023 —
left after 5 months, no results.
**Solution:** 90-day outbound program — ICP refinement, 3 email sequences,
LinkedIn outreach, weekly pipeline reviews, handoff playbook.
**Timeline:** 90 days
**Results:**
- $47K qualified pipeline generated
- 14 discovery calls booked (0 in prior 4 months)
- 3 deals closed-won ($31K combined)
- Cost per meeting: $340 (vs $2,100 with the failed BDR hire)
**Quote:** "We finally have a predictable way to fill the top of funnel
that doesn't depend on who my partners had coffee with last week."
```

**Phase 2:** One-pager, ~250 words. Headline: "$47K in New Pipeline for a B2B Consulting Firm in 90 Days." Snapshot: 3 metrics.

**Phase 4:** Saved to `northpeak-advisory-one-pager.md`. User requested follow-up long-form version for website.

## Example 4: Anonymous Client — Recovery Path (Short-Form)

**User says:** "I want to write up a project for a healthcare startup but they haven't given us permission to name them yet. We got their cost per lead from $180 to $42."

**Phase 1 extraction (anonymized):**

```
**Client:** A Series A digital health startup (B2C telehealth)
**Permission:** Pending — anonymize for now
**Contact:** VP of Growth
**Before:** Running paid acquisition with CPL of $180. Burning $30K/month
on ads with limited conversion tracking. Tried two ad agencies previously,
both missed CPL targets.
**Solution:** Full-funnel audit, landing page rebuild, conversion tracking
overhaul, creative testing framework.
**Timeline:** 10 weeks
**Results:**
- CPL: $180 to $42 (77% reduction)
- Monthly qualified leads: 165 to 710 at same spend
- Landing page conversion: 2.1% to 6.8%
**Quote:** According to the VP of Growth, the rebuild was the first time
their paid channel could scale without CPL drifting upward.
```

**Recovery applied:** Company name replaced with descriptor ("Series A digital health startup"). Contact replaced with role. Metrics and paraphrased quote retained. Note added: "If the client later approves attribution, I can update with their name and logo."

**Phase 2:** Short-form, ~450 words. Headline: "77% CPL Reduction for a Series A Digital Health Startup in 10 Weeks." Snapshot: 3 metrics.

**Phase 4:** Saved to `digital-health-cpl-case-study.md`. Flagged for update once naming permission is granted.
