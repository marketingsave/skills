# Preset: Welcome

## Objective
Turn a stranger into a fan within 7 days. Onboard new subscribers, buyers, or members. Every email must deliver value before asking for anything.

## When to Pick This Preset
- New email subscribers joining a list with no follow-up
- Customers completing a purchase who need onboarding
- Members signing up for a course, community, or membership
- Rebuilding a stale welcome flow that is not converting
- Launching a new offer that needs day-one automation

**Scope:** First 7 days only. Anything beyond day 7 → `nurture` preset. Post-purchase upgrade pitches → `upsell` preset.

## Typical Shape
- **Email count:** 5 emails (default) — cap at 7
- **Duration:** 7–14 days
- **Cadence:** Immediate, Day 1, Day 3, Day 5, Day 7

## Required Inputs (beyond universal)
1. What did the person just do? (subscribed, purchased, signed up)
2. What is the #1 thing you want them to do within 7 days?
3. What is their biggest fear or doubt right now?
4. What quick win can you deliver in email 1?
5. How many emails? (default 5)

**Minimum:** questions 1 and 2.

## Default Architecture

| # | Timing | Purpose | Subject Line Formula |
|---|--------|---------|----------------------|
| 1 | Immediate | Deliver the thing + set expectations | "Here's your [thing] + what's coming" |
| 2 | Day 1 | Quick win / first value | "Do this first (takes 5 min)" |
| 3 | Day 3 | Story + credibility | "How [person] went from [before] to [after]" |
| 4 | Day 5 | Overcome #1 objection | "The truth about [common doubt]" |
| 5 | Day 7 | Clear CTA / next step | "Ready for [next level]?" |

## Key Hooks / Angles Per Email

- **E1 (Immediate):** Deliver login / download / magnet. Set expectations for the next 6 days ("Here's what's coming"). The #1 predictor of completion is whether they take action in the first 24 hours.
- **E2 (Day 1):** One concrete thing they can do in under 5 minutes to get value. Not theory — action.
- **E3 (Day 3):** Case study or transformation story. Social proof beats feature lists.
- **E4 (Day 5):** Address the single biggest objection or fear (imposter syndrome, overwhelm, "I don't have time").
- **E5 (Day 7):** Clear CTA to the next step (paid offer, deeper content, community). Include unsubscribe-friendly language.

## Preset-Specific Constraints
- **Never put a sales pitch in email 1** — value only
- Each email must be under 300 words (mobile-first)
- Every email must stand alone valuable even if they never open another
- Include "reply to this email" in at least one email
- Email 1 should send within 5 minutes of signup

## Optimize Checklist
1. Email 1 delivers value with zero friction
2. Each email has ONE call-to-action
3. Each email references the previous (builds a sequence, not isolated blasts)
4. Conditional logic: if they clicked email 2 CTA, skip email 4
5. Email 5 includes unsubscribe-friendly language

## Key Metrics
- **Open rate email 1:** target > 50% (highest of any email you'll ever send)
- **Reply rate:** target > 2% on at least one email — primary deliverability signal
- **CTA click email 5:** 5–15% depending on offer

## Recovery
- **User does not know their #1 CTA:** Default to "reply to this email" for email 1 (builds engagement) and "consume the core content" for sequence goal.
- **User wants more than 7 emails:** Cap at 7. Offer to build a `nurture` sequence on top.
- **Low open rates on email 1:** Problem is almost always subject line or send timing. Email 1 must send within 5 minutes of signup.
- **No customer stories yet:** Replace email 3 with a "behind the scenes" email showing how the product/service was built and why.
