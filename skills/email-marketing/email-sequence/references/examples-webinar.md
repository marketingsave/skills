# Examples: Webinar Email Sequence

Three sample emails from a webinar campaign ("How to Land Your First $5K Freelance Client").

---

## Registration Email (Phase 1, Email 1)

```
Subject: how to land your first $5K freelance client (free training)
Preview text: The exact pitch that booked 38 calls last month

---

Hey [FIRST_NAME],

Next Tuesday at 1pm ET I'm running a free 60-minute training:

"How to Land Your First $5K Freelance Client (Without Cold DMs)"

Inside, you'll walk away with:
- The 3-line pitch that's booked 38 discovery calls in the last 90 days
- Why "lowering your rate to get experience" actually kills your pipeline
- The portfolio audit I do live with attendees (yours could be next)

This is for designers, writers, and consultants who can deliver
the work but are stuck under $2K projects.

Save your seat: [LINK]

Tuesday, [DATE] at 1pm ET (60 min + live Q&A)

Talk soon,
[YOUR NAME]

P.S. I'll send the slides + a bonus checklist to everyone who
shows up live. Replay attendees get the slides only.
```
