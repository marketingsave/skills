# Preset: Webinar

## Objective
Sell the webinar twice — once to get the registration, again to get them in the room. Registrations mean nothing if nobody shows up. Maximize registration, show-up rate, and post-webinar offer conversion.

## When to Pick This Preset
- Promoting a live or automated webinar
- Need registration page copy + confirmation emails
- Reminder sequences to maximize show-up rates
- Post-webinar follow-up for attendees AND no-shows (separate tracks)

**Do NOT use for:** podcast promotion, general event marketing, course launch emails (use `launch` preset).

## Typical Shape
- **Email count:** 8–11 emails across 5 phases
- **Duration:** 7–14 days pre-event + 3–5 days post
- **Trigger:** registration / campaign start

## Required Inputs (beyond universal)

| Input | What to Ask | Default |
|-------|------------|---------|
| **Webinar topic** | "What will it teach?" | No default |
| **Webinar date/time** | "When is live?" | No default |
| **Offer at end** | "Product pitch? What is it?" | Yes — product at end |
| **Target audience** | "Who should attend?" | Solopreneurs in the topic |
| **Registration page URL** | "Where do they sign up?" | Will provide copy |
| **Webinar platform** | "What tool?" | Zoom / platform-agnostic |

## Five-Phase Architecture

```
## Phase 1: Registration Drive (7-14 days before)
Email 1: Webinar announcement — what you'll learn + register CTA
Email 2: Content-led — quick win related to webinar topic
Email 3: Social proof angle — "Join 200+ who already registered"

## Phase 2: Pre-Webinar Reminders
Email 4 (Day before): Tomorrow reminder + what to prepare
Email 5 (Morning of): "We're live in 3 hours"
Email 6 (15 min before): "Starting now — join here"

## Phase 3: Live/During
Email 7 (During): "We're live — join now" (to non-attendees)

## Phase 4: Post-Webinar (Attendees)
Email 8 (Same day): Replay + offer recap
Email 9 (Day +1): Deeper dive on the offer + bonus
Email 10 (Day +2): Objection handling
Email 11 (Day +3): Deadline/last chance for offer

## Phase 5: Post-Webinar (No-Shows)
Email 8b (Same day): "You missed it — here's the replay"
Email 9b (Day +1): Key takeaways + offer
Email 10b (Day +3): Replay expiring soon
```

## Writing Rules by Phase

### Registration Emails (Phase 1)
- **Subjects** sell the outcome, not the format ("Learn X" not "Join our webinar")
- **Body** focuses on transformation: what they'll know/do after
- **Registration CTA** appears at least twice per email
- Include **date, time, timezone, duration** in every registration email

### Reminder Emails (Phase 2)
- Keep short — 50–100 words
- Join link prominent
- Day-before: one compelling reason to show up
- Day-of: just link and time

### Post-Webinar Emails (Phases 4–5)
- Attendee emails reference specific moments from the webinar (timestamps for replay)
- No-show emails lead with "Here's what you missed" + replay link
- Offer emails: same structure as sales emails (benefit, proof, objection, CTA)
- Replay deadline creates real urgency (48–72 hour window)

## Show-Up Rate Optimization
- Add calendar invite attachment to confirmation email
- Send 3 reminders minimum (day before, morning of, 15 min before)
- Include a "quick win" in the day-before email to build anticipation
- **Target 30–40% show-up rate** from registrations

## Registration Page Copy Outline
1. Headline (the outcome, specific)
2. Subheadline (who it's for + time investment)
3. 3–5 bullets of what they'll learn
4. Speaker bio snippet (credibility)
5. Registration form placement (above the fold)

## Key Metrics
- **Registration rate from emails sent:** target > 3%
- **Show-up rate:** attendees / registrations — target 30–40%
- **Replay watch rate:** target > 40% of no-shows
- **Offer conversion:** attendees convert at 2–5x no-shows typically

## Preset-Specific Anti-Patterns
- **No reminder emails.** Most no-shows simply forgot. Three reminders can double show-up.
- **Selling the webinar format.** "Join our webinar" is not a benefit. Sell what they will learn/achieve.
- **Same replay email to everyone.** Attendees and no-shows need different messaging.
- **Replay available forever.** No urgency to watch. Set a 48–72 hour window.
- **Pitching in every email.** Registration drive emails sell the *webinar*, not the product. Save the pitch for post-webinar.

## Recovery
- **No offer to pitch:** Build a pure value webinar. Replace post-webinar sales with a nurture sequence that deepens the relationship.
- **Webinar in 3 days (no time for registration drive):** Skip registration phase. Send 2 registration emails + 3 reminders. Focus on show-up rate.
- **Automated/evergreen webinar:** Adjust sequence to trigger on registration instead of calendar dates. Remove "live" language.
- **Low registration numbers:** Add a lead magnet bonus for registering, or a "bring a friend" incentive in reminder emails.
