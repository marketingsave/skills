Intent:              Informational (commercial-adjacent)

Launch order:        Pillar-first (existing site, DR 42, authority priority)

## Interlinking highlights

- **Hub → spoke:** all 12 linked from the pillar's chapters block, one descriptive anchor each.
- **Spoke → hub:** first paragraph of every cluster, anchor alternates between "email marketing", "a complete email marketing guide", and "our email marketing playbook".
- **Spoke ↔ spoke dense pairs:** (#2, #3) list-building feeds segmentation; (#5, #6) both are lifecycle sequences; (#1, #4) automation requires deliverability; (#10) is linked from #1 and #4 as the tool pick that executes them.
- **No link:** #7 does not link to #10 (subject lines → tools is a weak adjacency and would feel templated).
