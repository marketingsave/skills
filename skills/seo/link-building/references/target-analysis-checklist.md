# Target Analysis Checklist

## Prospect Scoring Rubric (1-5 per dimension, 25 max)

1. **Topical relevance** — 5 = exact niche match; 3 = adjacent; 1 = loose
2. **Organic traffic quality** — traffic to pages semantically related to your target
3. **Link velocity** — is the site actively publishing + earning links in last 6 months?
4. **Spam cleanliness** — inverse of toxic-link ratio (check referring domains)
5. **Accessibility** — editor/author contact findable; has published guest content or accepts pitches

Tiers: A >= 20, B 14-19, C 10-13, drop <10.

## Competitor Intersect Workflow (Ahrefs)

1. Identify 3-5 direct competitors ranking for target keywords
2. Ahrefs → Link Intersect → add competitors, exclude your domain
3. Filter: DR 20-80, dofollow, one link per domain, language match
4. Export → dedupe → manual review for relevance

## Spam Signal Flags (disqualify)

- Referring domains dominated by .xyz/.top/.cn spam TLDs
- Outbound link ratio > 100 per page (link farm)
- Recycled content / scraped posts
- DR inflated disproportionate to traffic (DR 60 + 200 monthly visits)
- Footer/sidebar link stuffing to unrelated commercial sites
- Ownership opacity (whois masked + no author bios + no about page)

## Contact Discovery

- Hunter.io, Apollo, Clearbit for editor/author emails
- LinkedIn Sales Navigator for editor-in-chief / content lead
- Check site's `/write-for-us`, `/contribute`, `/about` pages
- Twitter/Mastodon for warm intros before cold email
