# Examples

## Example 1: `full-mix` — 2-Week Calendar for a Business Coach (LinkedIn + Instagram)

```
Week 1:
Mon: [LinkedIn] Tip — "3 pricing mistakes costing you revenue" (text post)
Tue: [Instagram] Behind the scenes — recording a coaching call setup (photo + caption)
Wed: [LinkedIn] Personal story — "Why I stopped offering free consultations" (text post)
Thu: [Instagram] Carousel — "5 signs you need a business coach" (7 slides)
Fri: [Both] Client win — testimonial graphic + story

Week 2:
Mon: [LinkedIn] Hot take — "You don't need a business plan" (text post)
Tue: [Instagram] Tip — Quick Reel or carousel with a pricing framework
Wed: [LinkedIn] Case study — client went from $5K to $15K months
Thu: [Instagram] Community — repost a follower's win
Fri: [Both] Promotional — coaching program CTA
```

## Example 2: `video-heavy` — 5-Day Sample for a Freelance Business Creator

```
Day 1 (Mon): Educational — "3 red flags in freelance contracts" (30 sec, talking head)
  Hook: "If you see any of these in a contract, run."

Day 2 (Tue): Personal — "The client that almost made me quit freelancing" (60 sec, story time)
  Hook: "I was ready to give up after this one client."

Day 3 (Wed): Trending — [Adapt current trending audio to freelance pricing situation] (15 sec)
  Hook: [Matches trending format]

Day 4 (Thu): Educational — "How I write proposals that close 60% of leads" (45 sec, screen share)
  Hook: "This proposal template has closed $200K in projects."

Day 5 (Fri): Promo — Client testimonial clip + CTA to book a call (15 sec)
  Hook: "My client went from $3K to $12K months. Here's what she said."
```
