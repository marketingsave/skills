# Mode: video-heavy

30-day short-form video plan (TikTok/Reels/Shorts) with hooks, trending adaptation, and batch-recording. Use when deliverable is video-first and batch-recordable.

## Brief — Required Inputs

| Input | What to Ask | Default |
|-------|------------|---------|
| **Platform** | "TikTok, Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts, or all three?" | All three (create once, post everywhere) |
| **Niche / topic** | "What is your content about?" | No default — must be provided |
| **Posting frequency** | "How many videos per week?" | 5 per week (weekdays) |
| **Video capability** | "Talking head, voiceover, screen share, text-on-screen?" | Talking head |
| **Content pillars** | "What are your 3-4 content themes?" | Will define if not provided |
| **Goal** | "What should videos drive? Followers, website traffic, product sales?" | Followers and authority |

**GATE: Confirm brief before building calendar.**

For each video:

```

**Pillar:** [Educational / Personal / Trending / Promo]
**Format:** [Talking head / Text overlay / Tutorial / etc.]
**Hook (first 3 sec):** "[Exact opening line]"
**Topic:** [What the video is about]
**Key point:** [The ONE takeaway in 1 sentence]
**Duration:** [15 / 30 / 60 seconds]
**Text overlay:** "[Key text that appears on screen]"
**Audio suggestion:** [Trending sound, original audio, or music style]
**CTA:** [Follow, comment, visit link, save]
**Repurpose note:** [Can this be a carousel? A tweet? A newsletter snippet?]
```

## Write — Batch Recording Guide

```
**Session 1 (Week 1 content):** Record 5 videos in one sitting
- Set up once (lighting, camera, background)
- Change shirt between videos for variety
- Record all talking head videos first, then screen shares

**Session 2 (Week 2 content):** Record 5 videos
**Session 3 (Week 3-4 content):** Record 10 videos

**Total recording time:** 3-4 hours for 20 videos
**Editing:** 10-15 minutes per video for cuts and text overlays
**Scheduling:** Upload and schedule all at once using platform native schedulers
```

### Equipment Checklist
- Smartphone (modern phone camera is sufficient)
- Ring light or natural window light
- Phone tripod or mount
- Lapel mic or phone mic (quiet room)
- Simple background (clean wall, bookshelf)

## Write — Trending Format Adaptation

1. Check TikTok Discover page and Reels trending audio daily
2. Save trends that can be adapted to your niche
3. Adapt within 48 hours — trending formats have a 1-2 week window
4. Keep 2-3 "flex days" in the calendar for spontaneous trends
5. When adapting: keep the format, change the content to your niche

**Evergreen formats that always work:**
- "3 things I wish I knew about [topic]"
- "POV: [relatable situation in your niche]"
- "Stop doing [common mistake]. Do this instead."
- "Things that just make sense for [your audience]"
- "Replying to @[comment about your niche]"

## Anti-Patterns (video-heavy specific)

- **Planning without batch recording** — recording one video per day is unsustainable. Batch or fail.
- **Every video is a talking head** — vary formats: screen share, text overlay, b-roll, trending audio clips.
- **No hooks** — "Hey guys, so today I want to talk about..." loses viewers in 1 second.
- **Ignoring trends entirely** — trending formats get algorithmic boost. Reserve ~20% of content for trend adaptation.
- **Over-polished production** — short-form rewards authenticity over production value. Phone camera is fine.
