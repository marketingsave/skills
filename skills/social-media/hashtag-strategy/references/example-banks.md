# Hashtag Strategy — Worked Examples

Two end-to-end examples showing the full output format. Use as templates when delivering a strategy.

---

## Example 1: Fitness Coach on Instagram

**Input:** "I'm a fitness coach for busy moms. I post workout tips, healthy recipes, and mindset content on Instagram. I have about 3,200 followers."

**Output:**

# Hashtag Strategy: FitMom Coaching (Instagram)

**Account size:** 3,200 followers
**Platform:** Instagram
**Recommended per post:** 10-12 hashtags
**Tier mix:** 0 mega / 2 broad / 4 mid / 3 niche / 1 micro-niche / 1 branded

**Branded hashtag:** #FitMomWithSarah

---

## Content Pillars

1. **Workouts** — Quick home workouts for busy moms
2. **Nutrition** — Healthy meals and meal prep
3. **Mindset** — Motivation, self-care, mental health for moms

## Branded Hashtag Guidelines

**#FitMomWithSarah**
- Use on EVERY post as the last hashtag
- Encourage clients to use it when they share their own progress
- Feature posts that use the hashtag in your Stories weekly
- Goal: reach 500 posts within 6 months to establish community presence

## Example 2: Small Coffee Roastery on Instagram and TikTok

**Input:** "I run a small specialty coffee roastery. I post brewing tutorials, behind-the-scenes roasting, and coffee education. 870 followers on Instagram, 400 on TikTok."

**Output:**

# Hashtag Strategy: Ember Roasters

**Account size:** 870 (Instagram), 400 (TikTok)
**Platforms:** Instagram + TikTok
**Instagram recommended:** 10-11 hashtags per post
**TikTok recommended:** 3-5 per video
**Tier mix (Instagram):** 0 mega / 1 broad / 3 mid / 4 niche / 2 micro-niche / 1 branded

**Branded hashtag:** #EmberRoasters

---

## Content Pillars

1. **Brewing** — Pour-over, espresso, and brewing tutorials
2. **Roasting** — Behind-the-scenes roasting process
3. **Education** — Coffee origin stories, tasting notes, industry knowledge

## TikTok Hashtag Sets (3-5 per video)

### Brewing Tutorial
```
#coffeetok #pourover #homebarista #coffeebrewing
```

### Roasting BTS
```
#coffeetok #coffeeroasting #smallbusiness #behindthescenes
```

### Coffee Education
```
#coffeetok #coffeefacts #specialtycoffee
```

**TikTok note:** Always include #coffeetok — it is the primary community hashtag for coffee content on TikTok with billions of views. Keep total hashtags to 3-5. TikTok's algorithm relies more on content signals than hashtags, so fewer targeted tags outperform large sets.
