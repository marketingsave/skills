# Worked Examples

## Generic: Freelance Web Designer

```
Primary: LinkedIn (B2B clients, decision-makers)
Secondary: Instagram (portfolio, visual proof)
Skip: TikTok (audience mismatch), Twitter (low ROI)

Pillars:
1. Design tips (40%) — quick website improvements
2. Client wins (30%) — before/after, testimonials
3. Behind the scenes (10%) — process, tools
4. Promotional (20%) — services, availability

Cadence: LinkedIn 4x/week, Instagram 3x/week
Time: 5 hrs/week (2 creating, 2 engaging, 1 scheduling)
```

## LinkedIn: Fractional CMO

```
Headline: "Fractional CMO for B2B SaaS | Helped 12 startups hit $1M ARR | Follow for growth marketing tactics"
Pillars: Growth tactics (40%), Startup lessons (30%), Marketing hot takes (20%), Client wins (10%)
Cadence: Mon/Wed/Fri at 8 AM ET
Engagement: 30 min/day (10 commenting, 10 replying, 10 connecting)
90-day goal: 500 new connections, 5 inbound leads/month
```
