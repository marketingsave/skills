# YouTube Mode — Full Preset

Use this when `mode=youtube` is active. Replaces the generic Phase 3 Write steps.

## Outline (YouTube)

```
1. Channel Positioning — niche, value prop, ideal viewer
2. Content Pillars — 3-4 video categories
3. Upload Schedule — cadence and best publish times
4. Title & Thumbnail System — templates for consistency
```

## Y3. Title & Thumbnail System

```
## Title Formulas
1. "How to [Result] in [Timeframe] ([Qualifier])"
2. "[Number] [Things] I [Did] to [Get Result]"
3. "Why [Common Practice] Is Wrong (Do This Instead)"
4. "[Result] in [Timeframe] — Here's Exactly How"
5. "I Tried [Thing] for [Time]. Here's What Happened."

### Title Rules
- Under 60 characters (no truncation)
- Front-load the keyword or hook
- Include a number or specific result
- Create a curiosity gap

## Thumbnail Guidelines
- Face with emotion (surprise, curiosity, excitement) — if on camera
- 3-5 words of bold text (different from the title)
- High contrast colors (bright on dark or dark on bright)
- Clean, uncluttered composition
- Consistent brand colors and font across all thumbnails
- Dimensions: 1280x720px (16:9)
- Test: unreadable at phone size → redesign
```

## Y4. Video Structure

```
**Hook (0-30s):** Value prop in first 10s. "In this video, you'll learn [outcome]." No long intros.
**Body:** Follow the hook's promise. Pattern interrupt every 2-3 min (cut, b-roll, visual change). Chapters for 8+ min videos.
**CTA (final 30s):** Single CTA — subscribe, next video, or link. Suggest a specific next video. End screen: subscribe + suggested video.

**Target length:**
- Tutorials: 8-15 min
- Listicles: 10-20 min
- Vlogs/BTS: 5-10 min
- Shorts: under 60 sec
```

## Y5. 6-Month Growth Milestones

```
| Month | Focus | Upload Pace | Target |
|-------|-------|-------------|--------|
| 1 | Find rhythm, test topics | 1/week | 100 subs |
| 2 | Double down on what works | 1/week | 300 subs |
| 3 | Optimize thumbnails & titles | 1/week | 600 subs |
| 4 | Add Shorts strategy | 1 long + 2 Shorts/week | 1,000 subs |
| 5 | Collaborate with similar channels | 1 long + 2 Shorts/week | 1,500 subs |
| 6 | Monetization push | 1 long + 2 Shorts/week | 2,000+ subs |
```

## Y6. Monetization Path

```
**Direct (requires 1K subs + 4K watch hours):** Ad revenue, memberships, Super Chats
**Business (day 1):**
- Product/service link in every description
- Mention offer naturally in videos
- YouTube as trust-building funnel: viewers → email list → customers
- "Pillar" videos that answer customers' top questions
```

## YouTube Anti-Patterns

- **Broad niche** — "Business tips" attracts nobody. "Freelance pricing for designers" attracts the right viewer.
- **Ignoring thumbnails** — a video nobody clicks is a video nobody watches.
- **Long intros** — "Hey guys, welcome back..." loses viewers in seconds.
- **Only chasing trends** — trending gets views; niche content builds an audience. Balance both.
- **No CTA** — ending without a next step wastes the attention you earned.

## YouTube Recovery

- **Camera shy:** Screen-share tutorials or voiceover first. Add face later.
- **No views after 20 videos:** Audit thumbnails and titles. If CTR < 4%, redesign.
- **Cannot commit weekly:** Batch-record 4 videos in one day/month. Edit and schedule across the month.
- **Out of topic ideas:** Search your niche on YouTube. Look at competitors' most popular videos. Use Google Trends and "People Also Ask."
- **Subscriber growth stalling:** Add Shorts — they drive subs faster than long-form.
