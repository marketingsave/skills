# LinkedIn Mode — Full Preset

Use this when `mode=linkedin` is active. Replaces the generic Phase 3 Write steps.

## Outline (LinkedIn)

```
1. Profile Optimization — headline, about, featured, experience, banner
2. Content Pillars — 3-4 themes for consistent posting
3. Posting Schedule — cadence, formats, best times
4. Engagement Strategy — comments, connections, DMs
5. Connection Growth — outreach templates
6. 90-Day Growth Plan
```

## L1. Profile Optimization

```
**Headline (220 chars max):**
[What you do] | [Who you help] | [Proof or CTA]
Example: "I help B2B SaaS companies close 30% more demos | Sales coach for 200+ AEs | Follow for daily sales tips"

**About Section (2,600 chars max):**
P1: Who you help and what result you deliver (hook)
P2: Your story — how you got here (credibility)
P3: What you believe about your industry (perspective)
P4: How to work with you + CTA (conversion)

**Featured Section:**
- Pin your best-performing post
- Link to your lead magnet or website
- Share a case study or portfolio piece

**Experience Section:**
- Results-focused descriptions, not job duty lists
- Include metrics: "Grew revenue from $X to $Y" not "Managed sales team"

**Banner Image:** 1584x396px, value prop or CTA, on-brand.
```

## L2. Content Pillars (LinkedIn ratios)

```
**Pillar 1: Expertise / How-to (40%)** — frameworks, tips, tactical advice. Text with bullet/numbered lists.
Example: "5 things I tell every new freelancer about pricing"

**Pillar 2: Personal Stories / Lessons (30%)** — failures, surprises, BTS. Storytelling + lesson.
Example: "I lost my biggest client last month. Here's what I learned."

**Pillar 3: Industry Opinions (20%)** — contrarian takes, predictions, hot takes.
Example: "Unpopular opinion: cold email is still the best B2B channel."

**Pillar 4: Promotional (10%)** — testimonials, launches, offers, case studies.
Example: "Just helped a client double their demo rate. Here's how."
```

## L3. Posting Schedule

```
| Day | Post Type | Format |
|-----|-----------|--------|
| Monday | Expertise tip | Text post with list |
| Wednesday | Personal story / lesson | Storytelling post |
| Friday | Industry opinion or engagement | Question or hot take |

**Timing:** 7:30-8:30 AM audience timezone (Tue-Thu strongest)
**Minimum:** 3 posts per week
**Format mix:** 70% text-only, 20% text + image, 10% carousels or documents
```

## L4. Engagement Strategy — 30 min/day

```
**Before posting (10 min):**
- Comment on 5-10 posts from target connections
- Substantive comments (3+ sentences, not "Great post!")
- Engage with people BEFORE you connect

**After posting (10 min):**
- Respond to every comment within 2 hours
- Ask follow-up questions to extend conversations
- Like comments to acknowledge engagement

**Outreach (10 min):**
- Send 5-10 connection requests with personalized notes
- Follow up with new connections via value-first DM
```

## L5. Connection Outreach Templates

```
**New connection request (300 chars max):**
"Hi [Name], I noticed [specific thing — mutual connection, shared interest, recent post]. I share content about [topic] and would love to connect. — [Your name]"

**Welcome DM (after accept):**
"Thanks for connecting, [Name]! I noticed you're in [field]. I share [type of content] here — curious, what's your biggest challenge with [topic] right now?"

**NEVER** pitch in the first DM. Build rapport first.
```

## LinkedIn Anti-Patterns

- **Posting and ghosting** — no engagement with others kills reach. LinkedIn rewards conversation.
- **Pitching in DMs immediately** — gets you muted or reported.
- **Generic connection requests** — "I'd like to add you..." is ignored. Personalize every request.
- **Writing like a press release** — LinkedIn rewards authentic, personal. Drop the corporate tone.

## LinkedIn Recovery

- **Zero engagement:** Test formats (story vs tips vs question). Hook in first two lines. Engage more with others.
- **No content ideas:** Scroll feed 15 min. Riff on or respond to niche posts.
- **Uncomfortable with personal stories:** Start with a professional lesson or mistake. No life story required.
- **DMs not converting:** Build more touchpoints. Comment on their posts 3-5 times before DMing.
- **Time-strapped:** Batch-write Sunday evening. Schedule with native scheduler. Engage 15 min/day instead of 30.
