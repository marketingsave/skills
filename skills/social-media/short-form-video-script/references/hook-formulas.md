# Hook Formulas

Load this reference during Phase 2 (Structure) when picking the hook. Pick ONE per video.

| Hook Type | Structure | Best For |
|-----------|-----------|----------|
| **Curiosity gap** | "Nobody talks about this, but..." | Authority, how-to |
| **Bold claim** | "This one change doubled my [metric]" | Long-tail short-form, case studies |
| **Pattern interrupt** | Open with unexpected visual or sound | Entertainment, TikTok-native |
| **Direct address** | "If you're a [role] who struggles with [problem]..." | Niche audiences, tutorials |
| **Controversy** | "Unpopular opinion: [contrarian take]" | Engagement bait (use ethically) |
| **Result first** | Show the end result, then explain how | Transformation, tutorials |
| **Direct question** | Ask a question the audience cannot ignore | How-to content |

## Hook Rules

- **NEVER** start with "Hey guys, welcome to my channel" — instant skip
- **NEVER** start with "In this video, I'm going to..." — show, do not preview
- The hook must work with NO context — assume the viewer knows nothing about you
- First 1-2 seconds (tiktok/reels) or first 3 seconds (shorts) decide whether the viewer stays
- Test 3 different hooks for the same content — the hook determines ~80% of performance

## Expanded Examples

**Curiosity gap**
- "There's a reason your invoices are being ignored, and it's not the price."
- "Nobody tells new freelancers this about tax write-offs."

**Bold claim**
- "This one tweak doubled my email open rate."
- "I stopped doing sales calls and my revenue went up."

**Pattern interrupt**
- Snap fingers, smash a mug, flip the camera upside down — unexpected visual.
- Start mid-sentence: "...and that's why I'll never use Gmail again."

**Direct address**
- "If you're a solopreneur who can't get past $5k/month, watch this."
- "Freelancers — stop sending invoices this way."

**Controversy**
- "Unpopular opinion: niching down is overrated."
- "Most productivity advice is for people with easy jobs."

**Result first**
- "I went from 30 days to 5 days on payment turnaround. Here's how."
- Show the Notion board at inbox zero, then rewind.

**Direct question**
- "Why do 80% of freelancers fail in year one?"
- "What's the fastest way to get paid on a late invoice?"
