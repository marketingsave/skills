## Example 1: TikTok / Reels — "3 Invoice Tricks That Get You Paid Faster" (30s talking head)

```
**Preset:** multi
**Duration:** 30 seconds
**Format:** Talking head

### HOOK (0-3 sec)
[CUT TO: close-up, direct eye contact]
**"If clients always pay you late, you're making one of these three mistakes."**
[TEXT ON SCREEN: "Getting paid late? Try this"]

### SETUP (3-8 sec)
[CUT TO: medium shot]
**Spoken:** "I used to chase payments for weeks. Then I changed three things on my invoices and now I get paid within 5 days."
[TEXT ON SCREEN: "Went from 30+ days to under 5"]

### BODY (8-25 sec)
[TRANSITION: quick cut]
**Spoken:** "Number one — add a due date in bold at the top, not buried in fine print."
[TEXT ON SCREEN: "1. Bold due date"]
[TRANSITION: quick cut]
**Spoken:** "Number two — include a late fee line even if you never charge it."
[TEXT ON SCREEN: "2. Late fee line"]
[TRANSITION: quick cut]
**Spoken:** "Number three — send the invoice the second the work is done, not at end of month."
[TEXT ON SCREEN: "3. Send immediately"]

### PAYOFF (25-28 sec)
**Spoken:** "That's it. Three changes, paid in five days."
[TEXT ON SCREEN: "Paid in 5 days"]

### CTA (28-30 sec)
[CUT TO: tight shot, point at camera]
**Spoken:** "Follow for more freelance money tips."
[TEXT ON SCREEN: "Follow for more"]

---

**Audio suggestion:** Trending upbeat "business advice" sound or original voice with subtle background beat (90-110 BPM).
**Hashtags:** #freelance #invoicing #smallbusiness #getpaid #freelancetips #solopreneur #moneytips
**Caption:** Which one are you NOT doing? Drop the number in the comments.
```

## Example 2: Instagram Reel — "The 2-Minute Rule" (30s, high-energy)

```
**Preset:** reels
**Duration:** 30 seconds
**Format:** Talking head + B-roll montage

### HOOK (0-3 sec ~3s)
[CUT TO: tight shot, speaker snaps fingers]
**"You're procrastinating wrong."**
[TEXT ON SCREEN: "THE 2-MINUTE RULE"]

### BODY (3-22 sec ~19s)
[CUT TO: medium shot, fast and direct]
**Spoken:** "Here's the rule: if something takes less than two minutes — replying to that email, putting away the dishes, sending that invoice — do it NOW."
[TEXT ON SCREEN: "< 2 minutes? Do it NOW."]

**Spoken:** "Not later. Not after lunch. Right now."
[B-ROLL: rapid montage — typing email, filing paper, clicking "send" on invoice]

**Spoken:** "Because those tiny tasks pile up in your brain and drain your focus on the stuff that actually matters."
[TEXT ON SCREEN: "Small tasks pile up → big mental drain"]

### CTA (22-30 sec ~8s)
[CUT TO: tight shot, speaker points at camera]
**"Try it for one day. You'll feel the difference by noon."**
[TEXT ON SCREEN: "FOLLOW for daily productivity tips"]
**Spoken:** "Follow for more."
[SFX: upbeat stinger to end]

---

**Audio suggestion:** Trending productivity/motivational sound at 100-120 BPM, or licensed upbeat instrumental.
**Hashtags:** #productivity #2minuterule #timemanagement #procrastination #solopreneur
**Caption:** Save this one. The "2-minute rule" is the cheapest productivity hack that actually works.
```

**Shot list:** 2 camera positions (tight, medium) + 3 B-roll clips at 1.5-2s each. Estimated filming time: 10-15 minutes.
