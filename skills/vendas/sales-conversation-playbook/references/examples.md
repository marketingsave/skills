# End-to-End Examples

Three complete playbook examples across modes (inbound, outbound DM, upsell).

## Example 1 — Inbound Video Call, $2,000 Coaching Program

**Opening:**
"Hey [Name], thanks for jumping on. Before we dive in, let me tell you how this works so there are no surprises. I'll ask about where you are now and where you want to be, and if I think I can help, I'll share how we might work together. Sound fair?"

[Wait for yes. If they say "just tell me the price" — "Totally fair, I'll get to that. But I want to make sure it's actually the right fit first so neither of us wastes time."]

**Discovery (SPIN):**
- "What's your business right now and what are you selling?" [S]
- "What's working for you in terms of getting clients?" [S]
- "What's the biggest bottleneck — the thing that if solved, everything else gets easier?" [P]
- "How long has that been going on?" [P]
- "What has that cost you, roughly, in the last 6 months — revenue, time, or stress?" [I]
- "If we fixed that in the next 90 days, what would that mean for your business?" [N]

**Transition:**
"Okay, so what I'm hearing is [mirror their words]. And ideally you'd want [outcome]. That's exactly what we help people do."

**Pitch:**
"We run a 12-week program where we [deliverable 1], [deliverable 2], and [deliverable 3]. The reason it works is [differentiator]. For example, [client] came to us in a similar situation — [1-line case] — and within 90 days, [result]."

**Close:**
"The investment is $2,000, or 3 payments of $700. Based on what you told me about [pain point], do you want to grab a spot and start this week?"

[Silence.]

## Example 2 — Outbound DM Sequence, $5,000 Brand Packages

**DM 1:** "Hey [Name]! Been following your [thing] and love what you're building with [specific]. Quick question — happy with how your brand visuals represent the quality of what you do?"

**DM 2:** "Got it. I help [their type of business] level up their brand presence. Just wrapped a project for [similar business] — inquiry rate jumped 40% in month one. Open to a 15-min call?"

**DM 3 (3 days silent):** "No worries if timing isn't right! Dropped a case study on [similar business] rebrand — want me to send it over? No pitch, just thought you'd find it interesting."

**DM 4:** "Here's my calendar: [link]. Grab any 15-min slot. I'll come with 2-3 ideas specific to your brand so it's worth your time either way."

## Example 3 — Upsell Call, Existing $3k/mo Retainer → $7k/mo

**Opening:** Skip rapport. "Hey [Name], I want to spend 15 minutes on what's next for us. Cool?"

**Results-to-date:** "Since we started, we've hit [metric 1], [metric 2], [metric 3]."

**Pitch the expansion:** "The bottleneck now is [gap]. If we added [new scope], based on the current trajectory, we'd expect [projected result]."

**Handle "why now":** "You're right it's a bigger commitment. The reason I'm bringing it up now is [window / seasonality / data signal]. Waiting 6 months means [cost of delay]."

**Close:** "Want me to draft the updated agreement today?"
