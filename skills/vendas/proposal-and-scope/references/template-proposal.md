# Proposal Template (proposal mode)

Tone: **persuasive, confident, outcome-led**. The document argues for a yes.

```
# [Project Name] — Proposal for [Client Company]

Prepared by: [User's Name / Company]
Prepared for: [Client Contact Name], [Client Company]
Date: [Today's date]

---

## Executive Summary

[2-4 sentences. State the problem, the proposed solution, the primary
outcome, and the investment range. This is the only section some
decision-makers will read — make it count.]

---

## Problem Statement

[3-5 sentences. Describe the client's current situation, the pain
it causes, and why it needs to be addressed now. Use the client's
own language. Never blame the client. Connect to business impact.]

---

## Proposed Solution

[1-2 paragraphs. Your approach at a high level. Explain WHY this
approach works, not just WHAT you will do. Connect directly back
to the stated problem. One clear approach — no options here.]

---

## Deliverables

| # | Deliverable | Description | Timeline |
|---|-------------|-------------|----------|
| 1 | [Item] | [What the client receives] | [When] |
| 2 | [Item] | [What the client receives] | [When] |
| 3 | [Item] | [What the client receives] | [When] |

---

## Investment

[Use the pricing framework — see references/pricing-frameworks.md.
ALWAYS present pricing AFTER deliverables so the client
understands what they are paying for before seeing the number.
Frame as "investment," not "cost" or "price."]

---

3. Sign and return to begin the project on [proposed start date]
```

## PROPOSAL Section-by-Section Rules

- **Executive Summary:** Write LAST. Must stand alone. Include investment amount. 2-4 sentences max.
- **Problem Statement:** Client's own words. Never blame. Connect to business impact.
- **Proposed Solution:** Lead with outcome, not process. Avoid tool/tech lists. One clear approach.
- **Deliverables Table:** Every item is a tangible output. No "consulting hours" as deliverables.
- **Investment:** After deliverables. Clean numbers ($5,000 not $4,850). Frame as "investment."
- **Timeline:** Concrete milestones, not vague phases. "Design mockups delivered" not "Design phase."
- **Terms:** Default 50/50 for <$10K; 50/25/25 for >$10K. 2 revisions. 14-day validity.
