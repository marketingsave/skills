# SOW Template (sow mode)

Tone: **precise, contractual, boundary-focused**. The document prevents disputes.

```
## SCOPE OF WORK

**Project:** [Project name]
**Client:** [Client name]
**Provider:** [Your name/business]
**Date:** [Date]
**Version:** [1.0]

---

### 1. PROJECT OVERVIEW

[2-3 sentences. Project objective, context, and expected outcome.
Business goal, not tactical details. Reference the accepted
proposal if applicable.]

### 2. DELIVERABLES

| # | Deliverable | Description | Format | Due Date |
|---|------------|-------------|--------|----------|
| 1 | [Name] | [Specifically what it includes] | [PDF, Figma, code, etc.] | [Date] |
| 2 | [Name] | [Specifically what it includes] | [Format] | [Date] |
| 3 | [Name] | [Specifically what it includes] | [Format] | [Date] |

### 3. MILESTONES AND TIMELINE

| Phase | Description | Duration | Completion Date |
|-------|-------------|----------|----------------|
| Phase 1: Discovery | [Activities] | [X] weeks | [Date] |
| Phase 2: [Name] | [Activities] | [X] weeks | [Date] |
| Phase 3: [Name] | [Activities] | [X] weeks | [Date] |

### 5. EXCLUSIONS

### 6. ACCEPTANCE CRITERIA
```

## SOW Section-by-Section Rules

- **Project Overview:** Reference the accepted proposal. Business outcome in 2-3 sentences.
- **Deliverables:** Every item specific, measurable, with format and due date. "Website design" is NOT acceptable — specify page count, functionality, content responsibility.
- **Milestones:** Align with payment schedule. Every phase has completion date.
- **Assumptions:** Document EVERYTHING that must be true. Client-provided content, access, response times, third-party dependencies.
- **Exclusions:** Think about what the client might assume is included. List it. This section is the shield.
- **Acceptance Criteria:** Deemed-accepted clause (default 5 business days) is non-negotiable — it protects you from ghosting.
- **Fees:** Never 100% on completion. Always an upfront deposit (default 50%).
- **Change Orders:** Mandatory. Written, signed, may affect fee and timeline.
- **Sign-off:** Both parties sign with dates.
