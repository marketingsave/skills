# End-to-End Examples

## Example 1 (PROPOSAL mode): Web Design for a Bakery ($5K-$8K, Tiered)

**User says:** "I need a proposal for Sweet Crumb Bakery. Owner Lisa Martinez. New website. Budget $5K-$8K. Referral."

**Phase 1 produces:** Client = Lisa Martinez, Sweet Crumb Bakery. Problem: outdated one-page Wix site losing 5-10 catering leads/month — no catering menu, no inquiry form. Tried self-editing, gave up. Success: professional site, full menu, inquiry form, mobile-friendly, self-editable. Deliverables: 5-page site, inquiry form, Maps, SEO. Timeline: 6 weeks. Pricing: tiered.

**Phase 2a produces:**

- Executive Summary: "Sweet Crumb Bakery needs a professional website that showcases your full menu, captures catering inquiries, and gives corporate clients confidence in your brand. We will design and build a 5-page site with a catering inquiry system, mobile-responsive design, and a CMS you can update yourself. Investment ranges from $5,000 to $7,500 depending on the package."
- Deliverables: 6 items (wireframes, mockups, dev, form, content upload, launch).
- Tiered investment: Foundation $5K / Growth $6.5K (recommended) / Scale $7.5K.
- Timeline: 5 phases across 6 weeks.
- Terms: 50/50, 2-3 revision rounds, $200/extra, 14-day validity.

Saved to `sweet-crumb-bakery-website-proposal.md`.

## Example 3 (SOW mode): Post-Accepted Website Build

**User says:** "Sweet Crumb accepted the proposal. Now I need the SOW before we start."

**Phase 1 produces (SOW-specific additions):**
- Exclusions: ongoing maintenance after launch, copywriting (client provides), photography, e-commerce beyond catering form.
- Revisions: 2 rounds per deliverable, consolidated feedback counts as one round.
- Acceptance: written email approval; 5 business days deemed-accepted.
- Client dependencies: logo files (Week 1), menu content (Week 2), product photos (Week 3).
- Change-orders: written, signed, may affect fee and timeline.

**Phase 2b produces:**

- Project Overview: references accepted proposal dated [date], goal = launch professional site capturing catering inquiries.
- Deliverables: 6-row table, each with format (Figma, WordPress build, etc.) and specific due date.
- Milestones: 4 phases aligned with 3-payment schedule.
- Assumptions: feedback within 3 business days, logo/content delivered on stated dates, hosting on client's existing provider.
- Exclusions: explicit list (maintenance, copy, photography, e-commerce).
- Acceptance criteria: written approval OR 5-business-day deemed-accepted window.
- Fees: $6,500 total, 50/25/25 schedule, 1.5%/mo late fee.
- Change-orders clause: mandatory written change order.
- Sign-off: both parties.

Saved to `sweet-crumb-bakery-website-sow.md`.
